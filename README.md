# 🌾 Smart Mandi – Direct Farmer-to-Buyer Marketplace

Smart Mandi is a full-stack agricultural marketplace platform that connects farmers directly with buyers, reducing dependency on intermediaries and improving market accessibility. The platform enables farmers to list crops, communicate with buyers, view market insights, and manage their agricultural products through an intuitive web interface.

## 🚀 Live Demo

**Live Application:** https://smart-mandi-project.vercel.app/

## 📌 Problem Statement

Farmers often struggle to find buyers at fair prices due to the involvement of multiple intermediaries. This leads to reduced profits, limited market reach, and inefficient communication.

Smart Mandi addresses this challenge by providing a digital marketplace where:

* Farmers can directly showcase and sell crops.
* Buyers can discover available products easily.
* Both parties can communicate through an integrated chat system.
* Market insights help users make informed decisions.
